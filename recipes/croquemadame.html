Croque Madame

Description

A French classic sandwich with ham, cheese, and a fried egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 slices ham
  • 1/2 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp béchamel sauce

Steps

1. Butter one side of each bread slice.

2. Place ham and cheese inside and close sandwich.

3. Toast in a pan until golden brown.

4. Fry an egg separately and place on top.

5. Pour béchamel sauce over the sandwich and serve.
