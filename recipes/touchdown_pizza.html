Touchdown Pizza

Description

A cheesy, meaty pizza perfect for game day.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup black olives

Steps

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).

2. Roll out pizza dough and spread tomato sauce evenly.

3. Add mozzarella cheese, sausage, pepperoni, and olives.

4. Bake for 15-18 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
