Asparagus and Eggs

Description

Asparagus and eggs make the best spring breakfast or a beautiful light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 8 thin-stemmed asparagus stalks, trimmed
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

1. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.

2. Add asparagus and cook for 4 minutes.

3. Crack eggs over asparagus and cook until desired.

4. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
